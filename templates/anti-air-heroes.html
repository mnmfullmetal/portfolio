Anti Air Heroes

Role: Assistant Developer

Description: Anti Air Heroes is a fast-paced, 2D tap-to-shoot mobile game developed for Android. Players are tasked with defending the ground by tapping to shoot down a barrage of falling bombs. With each successful interception, players earn points and push for a new high score. The challenge intensifies as the game continues, and if three bombs manage to hit the ground, the round is over. Points earned during gameplay can be used to unlock a variety of cosmetic items.

  • Simple & Addictive Gameplay: An intuitive tap-to-shoot mechanic makes the game easy to pick up and play, perfect for short, engaging sessions.
  • High-Score Chase: The core objective is to survive as long as possible against the endless onslaught of bombs to achieve the highest score.
  • Unlockable Cosmetics: A point-based reward system allows players to unlock new items and customize their game.
  • Designed for Mobile: Built specifically for the Android platform, offering a classic arcade experience on the go.

In 2018, I had the valuable opportunity to contribute to this indie project as an assistant developer for a brief period. My responsibilities included assisting the lead developer with implementing core gameplay mechanics and refining the user experience. Although my involvement has since concluded, it was a rewarding experience to be part of an indie development team, and I remain a supporter of the project's future success.
